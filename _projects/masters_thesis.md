## Overview

This project develops a **multi-objective mixed-integer linear programming (MILP) framework**
to compare **additive manufacturing (AM)** and **traditional manufacturing (TM)**
under cost, time, and uncertainty constraints.

The work focuses on **aerospace spare parts production**, explicitly modeling:
- economies of scale,
- production lead times,
- and stochastic variability in demand, costs, and processing times.
